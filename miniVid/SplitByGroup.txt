To convert the embedded video HTML to the video's URL, you need to extract the `src` attribute from the iframe and convert it to the standard YouTube URL format. Here's how you can do it:

Given the HTML:
```html
<iframe width="355" height="200" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/JC-mrUjnfOs" title="YouTube video player" frameborder="0" allow="accelerometer; autoplay; clipboard-write; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture; web-share" referrerpolicy="strict-origin-when-cross-origin" allowfullscreen></iframe>
```

The converted video URL would be:
```
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=JC-mrUjnfOs
```

This URL can be used to directly access the video on YouTube.
