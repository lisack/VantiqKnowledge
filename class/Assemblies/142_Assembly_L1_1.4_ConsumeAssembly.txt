**Summary:**  
This video tutorial, titled "Consuming Assemblies," guides users on how to modify rules to view assemblies from a consumer's perspective rather than the author's. It covers updating an assembly to the catalog, configuring settings in the subscribing namespace, and making changes to the assembly's properties. The video also demonstrates how to create and use a custom app component, update assemblies, and run them in the consumer's namespace. It emphasizes the usefulness of assemblies for projects that need to be shared without exposing all implementation details.
