### Summary

This content discusses the importance of retrieving coherent responses from vectorized information using the Gen AI Builder. It highlights the challenges of document retrieval, particularly when dealing with numerous documents on the same topic, such as sulfuric acid. The Gen AI Builder offers several components to enhance semantic index responses, including a Semantic Index with compression and various compressor components. These compressors work with large language models to refine and rank document responses. The document also explains the use of rerankers and the RAG component, which formulates answers by searching through a semantic index and leveraging the LLM's training.
