Guardrails are tools designed to intercept and redirect inappropriate AI interactions, ensuring that applications maintain professionalism and ethics. The document explains the use of two components: Guardrails AI and NVIDIA Nemo Guardrails. Guardrails AI is simpler to implement, while Nemo Guardrails offers more precise control over user and LLM interactions. The Gen AI Builder has two components for using Guardrails from open source, Guardrails AI and NVIDIA Nemo Guardrails.
