Vantiq Integration In this video, we'll go through a list of features available on Vantiq for integrating with anything and everything on the Internet.

The whole point of real time event processing is that those events should be able to come from anywhere into the Vantiq system without hindrances that could slow processing.

Likewise, if the Vantiq platform interacts with other systems doing their own processing, Vantiq should be able to integrate those results into the platform seamlessly.

This is the essence of what we mean when we discuss integration.

There are multiple Vantiq features and tools to facilitate integration with the platform.
