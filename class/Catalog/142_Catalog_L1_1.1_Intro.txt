**Summary:**  
This video introduces the concept of Vantiq Catalogs, which are essential for building flexible distributed systems and sharing reusable projects while maintaining security. Catalogs act as namespaces dedicated to brokering events and resources between project namespaces. The video explains how to create and connect namespaces to catalogs, emphasizing the importance of catalogs in preventing event loss during failures. It also provides a step-by-step guide on setting up a catalog, creating access tokens, and connecting namespaces. The tutorial is recommended for gaining hands-on experience with Vantiq Catalogs.

Catalogs are a great way to build flexible distributed systems and share reusable projects while still maintaining total security over the system.

Catalogs are name spaces that are dedicated to brokering events and resources between project name spaces.

Catalog namespaces themselves should generally not have their own projects on them as this would adversely affect performance.
