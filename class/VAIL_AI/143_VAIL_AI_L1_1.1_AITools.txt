**Summary:**

This content provides an overview of how AI tools can enhance the capabilities of large language models (LLMs) by allowing them to perform beyond their regular abilities, especially in real-time situations. It explains how developers can create tools using VAIL procedures in Vantiq, which the LLM can evaluate and invoke as needed. The document also discusses the importance of providing descriptions for parameters and conditions for tool invocation, and how to programmatically inform the LLM about available tools. Additionally, it covers the concept of function authorizers, which are procedures that determine whether a tool should be executed, ensuring that tools are used appropriately. The content includes an example of a price check tool and demonstrates how tools can be integrated into AI applications to provide timely and relevant information.
