### Summary

The Vantiq callback service is a crucial tool for facilitating communication between services and clients, particularly in AI applications involving conversation widgets. Although not technically an AI service, it plays a significant role in AI conversations by enabling communication between a conversation widget on a client and a VAIL procedure in a service. The service includes procedures like `register` for setting up event pathways, `invoke` for sending data, and handling responses. The callback service is particularly useful for soliciting user information during AI conversations, even though it is not inherently an AI operation.
